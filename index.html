  • About Me

    My name is Ron Helfman im 29 years old from Herzliya.

    I served in the IDF as an electrical tank engineer in Givat and was awarded an outstanding soldier certificate for my service and participation in the "Tzuk Eitan" war.

    As part of a program for outstanding soldiers, I received a scholarship to study software testing at Sela College. I successfully completed my studies and was awarded an ISTQB international software tester certificate. After school and considering my young age at the time, I decided to travel the world and have new experiences before entering the world of software testing.

    I traveled to Australia and experienced the most amazing beaches in the world. I traveled to India and learned about one of the oldest and most colorful cultures in the world, And every year I fly for a snowboarding vacation of about a week with family or friends. Today I work in a chef's kitchen as a cook and I really enjoy my work, but I am looking for something more challenging and meaningful. That's why I decided to study again, at the Automation College, to refresh my memory on software testing and keep up to date with the changes that have happened in recent years and in addition to study automation testing.

    As a child I used to play a lot of computer games and therefore I really enjoy working on a computer, I discovered that finding bugs and preventing bugs that could damage an entire system is also a lot of fun.
